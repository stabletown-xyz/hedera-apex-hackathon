Stabletown Munus Lite demonstrates compliant buyer-side municipal distribution workflows using a tenant-scoped control plane with Hedera-oriented semantics. The flow tracks offering setup, disclosure publication, investor allowlist approvals, purchase records, coupon payments, and transfer compliance checks while preserving strict audit and outbox guarantees. Signer-provider abstraction supports local and AWS KMS backends, enabling a practical custody narrative for issuer actions. The demo is intentionally sandbox-scoped: no production securities activation, but clear operational rigor with deterministic replay behavior for money-adjacent writes. Judges see a complete issuer workflow, policy enforcement touchpoints, and a roadmap to broader buyer distribution and underwriter collaboration without rewriting core platform contracts.
